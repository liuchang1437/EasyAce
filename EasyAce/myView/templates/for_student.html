FAQ

1. Q: How do I obtain tuition service from Easy Ace?

A: You can simply register by completing Student Registration Form.

2. Q: Can I get tuition without filling registration form?

A: Yes, you can leave your phone number on our webpage and we will contact you shortly, but try not to do so, go to our home wage and register a tutor.

3. Q: How do you charge?

A: This is our recommended price table. The final price is negotiable as long as the tutor and the student reach agreement. Generally, we recommend that tutors could charge S$5 less when students go to tutor’s place for tuition.

4. Q: Is there a trial lesson? What if I find the first allocated tutor unsuitable for me?

A: Yes, there is one trial lesson for each tuition case. If the first tutor allocated is unsuitable for you after the first lesson, we are willing to change the tutor for you. The first lesson conducted will not be charged. However, if you are satisfied with the first tutor and decide to continue, the first lesson will be chargeable.

5. Q: What if I find the second allocated tutor still unsuitable for me?

A: This is an unlikely situation. However, you can still request to change for another tutor from Easy Ace, but the lesson given by the second tutor remains chargeable.

6. Q: What is the Feedback Form for?

A: Filling a Feedback Form only takes 2 minutes. It allows us to know your opinion about the teaching quality so that we can provide better service for you and give due credit/punishment to your tutor.

7. Q: Why should I fill in the Feedback Form?

A: If you complete the feedbacks for at least one consecutive month, $20 vouchers will be awarded.

8. Q: What should I do if I want to reschedule, cancel or add a tuition session?

A: Student can settle with his or her own tutor. But within the first month, please acknowledge us through email or phone call for any cancellation or addition of lesson.

9. Q: How do I contact Easy Ace to get further assistance?

A: Student can email us at tuition@easyace.sg, or directly contact our Student Manager at 82654707 98110719.

10. Q: Will I be disturbed with excessive calls?

A: We will only call ONCE at the end of the first month to listen to your feedback on the learning experience. That’s all.
